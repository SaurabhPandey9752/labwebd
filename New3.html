Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Sliced bread

Instructions:

1. Place a skillet or frying pan over medium heat.

2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, onto the skillet.

4. Add a layer of cheddar cheese slices, followed by bacon and tomato slices.

5. Top with another slice of bread, butter-side up.

6. Cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted.

7. Remove from skillet and serve hot.
